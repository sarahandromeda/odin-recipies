Perfect Pumpkin Pie by Eagle Brand

Jump to Recipe....Source....Home

Description

Oh the Pumpkin Pie! A delicious dessert perfect for autumn. This pie will make your home smell amazing and give all your guests the feeling of home with each bite!

Ingredients

  • 15 oz of cooked, mashed pumpkin
  • 14 oz of condensed milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp ground ginger
  • 1/2 tsp ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 9 inch pie crust

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425 F.
  2. Whisk pumpkin, condensed milk, eggs, spices, and salt in bowl until smooth. Pour into crust.
  3. Bake for 15 minutes.
  4. Reduce oven temperature to 350 F and bake for 35-40 more minutes, until knife stuck into pie 1 inch from edge comes out clean.
  5. Let cool and garnish with whipped creap if desired.
